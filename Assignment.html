Courage

Mordecai has the heart of a lion. He always jumps in to defend anyone in need of help he does not care about his own safety but only about the one who he is trying to defend.

"Dachshund" is a German word meaning "badger dog," and the breed's German history goes back some 600 years. And, as the breed name suggests,the Dachshund was developed to enthusiastically dig his way into a badger den and dispatch its occupant. The Dachshund's long, low body was custom-made for this subterranean work.
